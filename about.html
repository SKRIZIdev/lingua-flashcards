About Lingua

Lingua is a tiny spaced-flashcard trainer for building vocabulary in Spanish, French and German.

How to study

  • Read the foreign word, guess the meaning, then Flip.
  • Hit Know it to retire the card, or Again to keep it in rotation.
  • Cards you don't know come back around until you've got them.

Everything runs offline — your progress lives only in this browser.
